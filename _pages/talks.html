The Future of Insurgencies and Radical Groups in Africa (July, 9, 2024)

A discussion featuring Dr. Tim Jones, a Donald R. Beall Defense Fellow in the Department of Defense Analysis at the Naval Postgraduate School, and Dr. Christopher Anzalone, a Research Assistant Professor with Middle East Studies and the Krulak Center for Innovation & Future Warfare at Marine Corps Universityon on the current status, activities, and potential future plans of radical insurgent groups like al Shabaab in Africa.
